DJ V-Lux – Press Kit

Artist Info

Genre: Allround DJ | Hard Techno | Industrial Techno
Origin: Mechelen, Belgium

Short Bio

DJ V-Lux is an allround DJ from Mechelen, Belgium, known for delivering high-energy sets that leave no dancefloor untouched. Active for over 5 years and now leaning heavily into hard and industrial techno, V-Lux is a rising force in the Belgian underground scene.

Long Bio

DJ V-Lux is a versatile and passionate DJ hailing from the vibrant region of Mechelen. Over the past five years, he's built a reputation as an allround performer, blending genres and reading the room with precision. But as of early 2025, V-Lux has found his true calling in the raw, driving sounds of hard and industrial techno - and he's not looking back.

Now also stepping into music production, DJ V-Lux is sharpening his sound with original tracks that reflect his intense and energetic style. His dynamic sets have electrified crowds at major Belgian events like Gladiolen, Soiree Tropicale, Weerdse Bierfeesten, College TD, and the underground parties of Cave28.

Whether it's an outdoor festival or a gritty rave cave, DJ V-Lux always brings fire with non-stop energy, dark kicks, and pounding rhythms. Expect chaos. Expect sweat. Expect movement.
