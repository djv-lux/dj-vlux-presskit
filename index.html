Short Bio

DJ V-Lux is an allround DJ from Mechelen, Belgium, known for delivering high-energy sets that leave no dancefloor untouched. V-lux is active for over 5 years now.

Long Bio

DJ V-Lux is a versatile and passionate DJ hailing from the vibrant region of Mechelen. Over the past five years, he's built a reputation as an allround performer, blending genres and reading the room with precision.

In the beginning of 2025 he also stepped into music production, specifically in the techno and house scene. DJ V-Lux is sharpening his sound with original tracks that reflect his intense and energetic style. His dynamic sets have electrified crowds at major Belgian events like Gladiolen, Soiree Tropicale, Weerdse Bierfeesten, College TD, and the underground parties of Cave28.

Whether it's an outdoor festival or a gritty rave cave, DJ V-Lux always brings fire with non-stop energy, dark kicks, and pounding rhythms. Expect chaos. Expect sweat. Expect movement.
