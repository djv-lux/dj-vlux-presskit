Short Bio

DJ V-Lux is an energetic allround DJ from Mechelen, Belgium known for delivering powerful sets that keep dancefloors moving. With over five years of experience behind the decks, he blends multiple genres into dynamic performances suited for festivals, clubs and parties.

Long Bio

DJ V-Lux is a versatile and passionate DJ who built a strong reputation within the Belgian nightlife scene. Known for reading the crowd and creating high-energy atmospheres, his sets combine powerful transitions, crowd interaction and a dynamic mix of genres.

In early 2025 he expanded his musical journey by stepping into music production, focusing mainly on house and techno. His productions reflect the same high-energy sound that defines his DJ sets.

Whether performing at festivals, student parties or underground raves, DJ V-Lux always delivers a memorable experience.
